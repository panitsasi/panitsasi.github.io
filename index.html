About Me

Hello! I am Ioannis, and I respond to both John and Yannis. Currently, I am in my second year of pursuing a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at Yale University. I feel privileged to be conducting research under the esteemed guidance of Professor Leandros Tassiulas at the prestigious Yale Institute of Network Science, particularly as a member of the SmartNets research group.

My primary areas of academic interest and research include Wireless Networking, Computer Security, and Artificial Intelligence.

I completed my undergraduate studies at the University of Patras in Greece, specializing in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, and graduated in 2021. During my mandatory military service, I attained the rank of second lieutenant in 2022 at the School of Military Engineering in Loutraki, Greece. Afterward, in 2023, I successfully completed my Master's of Science degree with honors in Electrical Engineering at Yale University.



Wireless Networking

In my research endeavors, I am integrating Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL) techniques to enhance Software Defined Networking (SDN) and achieve more efficient controller synchronization. By leveraging DRL, I aim to develop intelligent algorithms that enable controllers to learn and adapt their actions dynamically, optimizing network performance and responsiveness. This novel approach opens up possibilities for self-adaptive and autonomous SDN control, revolutionizing how networks are managed and operated.

Computer and Network Security

My research centers on creating robust malware detection systems for the Android Operating System by harnessing data from both static and dynamic analysis. I aim to leverage the potential of Deep Learning techniques to build powerful models capable of accurate detection and classification of malicious activities within application traffic.

Artificial Intelligence

My passion lies in Artificial Intelligence, with a specific focus on utilizing advanced algorithms to optimize network performance, reduce latency, and effectively monitor next-generation networks. Additionally, I leverage these algorithms to bolster security measures and safeguard critical infrastructure from potential threats.
