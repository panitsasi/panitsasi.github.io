About Me

Hello! My name is Ioannis or you can call me Giannis and i come from Patras, Greece. Currently, I am in my second year of pursuing a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at Yale University. I am working with Prof. Leandros Tassiulas at the Yale Institute for Network Science (YINS), and i am member of the SmartNets research group. My primary areas of academic interest and research include 5G/6G Networks, Security, and Machine Learning.
I completed my undergraduate studies at the University of Patras in Greece, majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering, and graduated in 2021, supervised by Prof. Dimitrios Serpanos. During my mandatory military service, I attained the rank of Second Lieutenant in 2022 at the School of Military Engineering in Loutraki, Greece. Afterwards, in 2023, I successfully completed my Master of Science degree in Electrical Engineering at Yale University.
